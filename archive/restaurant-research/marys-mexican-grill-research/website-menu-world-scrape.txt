Welcome To Mary's Mexican Grill

Mary's Mexican Grill, located in Woodstock, IL, offers an authentic Mexican dining experience infused with vibrant flavors. Renowned for its diverse selection of tacos, including mouthwatering options like Asada, Carnitas, and Lengua, each dish is crafted with care using fresh ingredients. The homemade tortillas elevate traditional meals like Chicken Enchiladas and Chimichangas. Guests rave about the zesty salsa and the flaky chips, making every bite delightful. With standout items like Shrimp Empanadas and flavorful Ceviche, Mary's promises a culinary adventure that transforms casual dining into a memorable feast. It's a local gem that deserves a visit for its heartfelt hospitality and delicious offerings.

About

Mary's Mexican Grill

Nestled in the heart of Woodstock, Illinois, Mary's Mexican Grill stands out as a culinary treasure for anyone craving authentic Mexican cuisine. Located at 108 Cass St, this vibrant eatery invites you to embark on a flavor journey that celebrates the rich traditions of Mexican cooking.

As you step inside, a warm and welcoming atmosphere envelops you, hinting at the flavors that await. Each dish is crafted with care, featuring fresh ingredients and a commitment to genuine taste. From the moment you glance at the menu, your taste buds will be tantalized by an extensive array of options that showcase both classic and innovative Mexican staples.

Among the highlights are the Burritos Suizos, which come adorned with suiza sauce and melted cheese, offering a delightful experience whether you choose shrimp, steak, or chicken. The Chimichangas are equally impressive, boasting a crispy exterior that encases generous portions of flavorful fillings. Notably, the asada and pollo options have garnered much love from patrons for their hearty taste and satisfying presentation.

One must not overlook the Antojitos or “Mexican cravings,” where you'll find treasures like tamales and sopes that evoke nostalgia for traditional home-cooked meals. Dishes like the Chicken Enchiladas Verde and the Chicken Fajitas have been rave-reviewed for their vibrant flavors and the love that goes into their preparation. The homemade tortillas are celebrated, and it’s no wonder—they encapsulate the freshness that diners seek in genuine Mexican fare.

Customer feedback underscores the restaurant’s commitment to excellent service and memorable meals. Both enriched by the deliberate preparation of fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors, dishes like the beef tacos with cilantro, onion, and lime have earned a dedicated following. Diners rave about the homemade guacamole and the irresistible salsa that accompanies each meal, with many expressing a desire to take these flavors home.

Whether you’re treating yourself to a weekday lunch or enjoying a joyful dinner with friends, Mary's Mexican Grill promises to deliver an experience bursting with authenticity and flavor. So why not take a moment to explore this gem? It's an invitation that every foodie should accept.
