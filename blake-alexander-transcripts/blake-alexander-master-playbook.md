## Master Playbook: Patterns & Step-by-Step System

### THE CORE MODEL (appears in every single video)
Sell local businesses without websites a monthly website subscription ($197–$397/month). Build once using Framer or GoHighLevel templates, duplicate for each client, own their domain, keep them dependent on you. Grow through cold calling. Scale through MRR compounding + sub-5% churn.

---

### STEP 1: SETUP (~1 hour, do once)

**Agency infrastructure:**
- Buy a domain for your agency: Namecheap (~$11–16/year)
- Create a logo: ChatGPT prompt for ideas → VidIQ ($1/month via vidiq.com/blake) to generate AI options → pick best, export black + white variants
- Build agency landing page: Framer free template ($5/month hosting) → paste into ChatGPT ("what should I change?") → update copy and images
- Payment processor: **Fanbase** (best automation, lowest fees), Stripe (most trusted by clients), or polar.sh (Stripe wrapper, less strict). Set up monthly recurring billing.

**Tech stack for client websites (pick one):**
- **Framer** — drag-and-drop, $5/month per client, easiest to use, no coding
- **GoHighLevel** — all-in-one CRM (website + SMS + AI chat + reviews + SEO), harder to set up but more powerful

---

### STEP 2: NICHE SELECTION (do once per niche)

**Rules:**
- Don't pick the top 3 most obvious niches (oversaturated)
- Pick niches with high AOV (average order value per job)
- Start with ONE sub-niche; become the best in that niche before expanding

**Best niches by AOV tier:**
- **Top tier** ($397/month): Roofing ($8–40K/job), Solar ($10–60K), HVAC ($5–25K)
- **Mid tier** ($297/month): Plumbing ($300–500/job), Electrical, Landscaping, Pest Control
- **Lower tier** ($197/month): Car detailing ($50–150/job), Cleaning, Locksmithing

**ChatGPT prompt for niche ideas:**
> "Give me a list of local service businesses similar to plumbers, electricians, and roofers, ranked by average job value and low competition for digital marketing."

**Sleeper picks repeatedly mentioned:** Locksmithing, septic/drainage, scaffolding, tiling, HVAC

---

### STEP 3: BUILD THE MASTER TEMPLATE (do once per niche)

1. Go to Framer → search "[niche] template" → find one you like (many are free; paid multi-niche bundle ~$39)
2. Rename to a placeholder client name
3. Add plugins (do this ONCE, carries over to every duplicated version):
   - **Contact form** (Framer plugins → search "contact form" → free options available)
   - **AI live chat** (Framer plugins → "live chat and AI" → trains on website content)
   - **Google Reviews embed** (paste their Google Maps URL → auto-fetches reviews)
   - **SMS follow-up** (GoHighLevel handles this natively)
4. Publish and test
5. This is now your master template — every client gets a duplicate of this

**Manual (free):**
- Google: search "[niche] near me" → scroll through Google Maps results → find businesses with NO website listed → save their phone number in a spreadsheet
- Target: 90+ businesses per session

**Automated (Tenji.ai):**
- Enter niche + suburb → generates leads ranked 1–100 (90+ = no website, high-probability close)
- Built-in CRM: drag leads through "Cold → Called → Interested → Followed Up → Closed"
- Website generator: enter lead name + niche → full Framer demo site in 5 seconds
- Script generator: enter lead name + website problems → full cold call script

**Lead quality rules:**
- Target businesses with NO website (not just bad websites — no website = easiest sell)
- Avoid businesses that already have strong digital presence
- Volume matters: aim for 150–400 cold calls per day

### STEP 5: COLD CALLING

**The golden rule: NEVER sell on the cold call. Only book the demo meeting.**

**Opener script (verbatim, used across every video):**
> "Hey, is this [Business Name]? ... I got your number from [area/referral]. I'm actually a web design student and I was working on a project — I mocked up a completely free demo website for you. No pressure at all. Whenever you have 5 minutes, I'd love to show you. When are you free this week?"

**Handling objections:**
- *"I already have a website"* → "I noticed it's not loading / I couldn't find it on Google. I built you something that might work better — it's free to look at."
- *"I've gotten 5 calls like this"* → "Most don't look high quality or are unresponsive. I already built yours — just 5 minutes to see it."
- *"Not interested"* → "No problem at all. Thanks for your time." (Move on immediately)

**Booking the call:**
- Send a Google Meet link or Cal.com booking link
- Demo should be same day or within 48 hours while interest is warm

**Volume benchmarks:**
- 150 cold calls/day → 10 booked demos → 5 closes → $1,000 MRR added
- 400 cold calls/day → 10–20 demos → 5–8 closes → $1,000–1,500 MRR added
- One grinding day compounds forever (MRR, not one-time)

**Pricing anchoring:**
- High AOV niches → start at $397/month, come down to $297 if needed
- Mid AOV niches → $297/month, firm
- Low AOV niches → $197/month
- Frame as "less than one job's profit per month"

### STEP 8: CHURN OPTIMIZATION (the real moat)

**Target: 5% monthly churn (industry average: 20–50%)**

**How to achieve it:**

1. **Own the domain** — never transfer it to the client. If they cancel, they lose the domain AND all SEO value built up. This alone reduces churn dramatically.
2. **Target non-technical businesses** — older owners who don't know how to DIY a website won't leave. They'd have to find someone else, explain everything from scratch, and lose continuity.
3. **Bundle sticky services** — SMS follow-up systems, email sequences, Google review management (filter 1–3 star reviews to go to the business privately, not public). Each added service raises exit friction.
4. **Never give backend access** — clients should never log in to Framer or GoHighLevel. You handle all updates. Perceived complexity = retention.
5. **Keep pricing at the "no-brainer" threshold** — $197–297/month is below the threshold where they'll bother shopping around.

**Churn math — why this compounds:**
- 100 clients × $297 = $29,700 MRR
- 5% churn = 5 cancels/month
- Need only 6 new closes/month to grow MRR net positive
- At 30% churn = 30 cancels/month needed to maintain MRR → requires constant hustle

### STEP 9: SCALING

**Phase 1 — 0 to $5K MRR (solo):**
- Cold call everything yourself
- Build templates yourself
- Manage Framer / GoHighLevel yourself
- Goal: prove the model works, understand every objection

**Phase 2 — $5K to $20K MRR:**
- Hire a VA on OnlineJobs.ph or Fiverr for: lead scraping, template building (NOT cold calling)
- Continue doing cold calls yourself or hire a local cold caller
- Hiring a cold caller: use Upwork, filter for local accent (first-world market = needs credible voice), male in 20s works best
- Pay structure: $400/day upfront OR 30–35% lifetime commission on every client they close
- Commission structure aligns incentives better at this stage

**Phase 3 — $20K+ MRR:**
- Run Meta ads ($400/month budget min) targeting local business owners in your niche
- Retarget from personal brand content
- Hire multiple cold callers (commission-based)
- VA handles all template work and delivery
- You focus on: hiring, finance, strategy, ad creative
- Commission cold caller: 35% lifetime revenue per client they close

**Scaling math (at $297/month):**
- 50 clients = $14,850 MRR (~$178K ARR)
- 100 clients = $29,700 MRR (~$356K ARR)
- 161 clients = $47,817 MRR (~$574K ARR)
- 170 clients = $50,490 MRR (~$606K ARR)
