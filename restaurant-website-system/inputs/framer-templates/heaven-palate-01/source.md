# Heaven Palate Template — EVALUATED AND REJECTED

- **Status:** **REJECTED — duplicate register, not a catalog fit**

## Why rejected

This template sits in the same register as `1776-redesign-01` (dark-mode warm-upscale fine-dining) but with less typographic discipline:

- **Palette:** dark canvas `#02110C` (near-black green) + cream-gold text `#FFEDBF` + gold accent. Same register family as 1776's navy+amber.
- **Display:** Playfair Display weight 700 at 128px — shouts rather than disciplines. 1776's Cormorant Garamond with italic-on-serif emphasis is a more refined execution of this register.
- **Copy register:** "A haven for discerning palates," "Exquisite Elegance, Culinary Delight," "Embark on a Gastronomic Adventure" — aspirational fine-dining, reservation-essential.
- **Primary CTA:** "Reserve Table" / "Make Reservation" — same reservation-essential conversion model as 1776.

## Why it doesn't address the real gap

Ethan's priority for template #6 is the **mid-tier casual** register (pizza / Mexican / Italian family / fast-casual takeout-heavy) where 60% of outbound prospects actually sit. Heaven Palate fails all 5 criteria for that register:

1. ❌ Dark palette — mid-tier casual needs light/bright
2. ❌ No product-card menu treatment — uses atmospheric photography instead
3. ❌ Fine-dining copy register — not "best pizza in town" energy
4. ❌ Reserve-Table primary CTA — mid-tier casual needs "Menu" or "Order"
5. ❌ Atmospheric photography — mid-tier casual needs dish-forward close-ups that trigger hunger
