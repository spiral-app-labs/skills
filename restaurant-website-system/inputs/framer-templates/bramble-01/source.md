# Bramble Template

- **URL:** https://bramble.framer.website
- **Type:** Framer template
- **Author / studio:** Dan Marek (https://dan-marek.framer.website)
- **License notes:** Framer marketplace template
- **Why we picked this:** Third template. Fills a completely different lane from `qitchen-01` (editorial-quiet-dark-fine-dining) and `1776-redesign-01` (editorial-warm-dark-upscale-destination). Bramble is **light-mode, retro, music-forward cocktail-bar-and-daytime-restaurant hybrid** — a whole new register. First light-mode template in the catalog. Also contributes a slate of reusable patterns: hero slideshow, polaroid strip, stacked-vinyl decorative element, equal-billing dual-service menus split, external-PDF-menu flow, section-level bg switching.
- **First impression vibe:** Retro, warm, hospitality-driven, music-forward, scrapbook-energy, Soho cocktail-bar character.
- **Likely archetype match:** Primary **retro-cocktail / music-forward bar / hospitality-casual**. Also fits **neighborhood restaurant with bar emphasis**, **daytime-food-nighttime-drinks hybrid venues**. Does NOT fit moody-speakeasy (Bramble is warm, not moody) or fine dining.

## Unique structural patterns observed
- **Hero slideshow with static wordmark overlay** — hero image cycles through 3-4 scenes at ~1s intervals (bar speakers → food spread → cocktails on bar → back to speakers), with massive "BRAMBLE" wordmark anchored at bottom persisting across all frames. Identity-constant + content-rotating.
- **Polaroid photo strip** — 5+ guest-photographed square photos with thin white borders, slight rotations, partial horizontal-bleed off-screen. Scrapbook/nostalgia aesthetic.
- **Horizontal display-serif marquee with repeated phrase ("OPENING TIMES · OPENING TIMES · ...")** — no bullet separator, just repetition. Variant of 1776's marquee.
- **Stacked-vinyl visual** — ~8 stacked near-black ellipse discs as section break, signaling music identity. Decorative SVG, not photo.
- **Equal-billing dual-service split** — "Food | [menu card image centerpiece] | Drinks" with "VIEW MENU" links on each side. For dual-concept venues (day/night).
- **Section-level bg switching** — hero=photo, polaroid strip=cream, menus=near-black, floral break=photo, gift/careers=near-black, mailing=cream, footer=cream. Intentional alternation signals the "multi-register" brand.
