## First Impression

The old site has real business engines and real content: Tock reservations,
Toast carry-out, Uber Eats delivery, Toast gift cards, iMenuPro menu content,
weekly wine/martini specials, and an established-2000 story. The visual and
conversion layer undersells those strengths: generic first-screen copy, a
bright cookie banner over the hero, dated Divi/Zara styling, and provider links
that feel bolted on instead of orchestrated.

## Secret Sauce Summary

The pasted Google reviews say Bistro Wasabi is remembered for fresh sushi,
inventive maki, martinis/wine, generous shrimp/scallop and steak entrees, an
elevated strip-mall atmosphere, and long-running consistency. Use that to shape
the fork.
