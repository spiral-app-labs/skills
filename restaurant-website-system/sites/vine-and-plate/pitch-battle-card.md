# Vine & Plate Pitch Battle Card

## Lead With

"I did not rebuild this as a rip-and-replace. Toast stays. Your reservation page stays. The difference is that guests can now understand the plates, wine nights, hours, and order/reserve paths before a PDF interrupts them."

## Before / After

| Current site | Redesign |
| --- | --- |
| Food and drink actions open PDFs. | Inline food + wine preview with PDF links still available. |
| Menu intent is not a crawlable page-native experience. | Dedicated `/menu` route creates a readable menu surface before PDF fallback. |
| Wine identity appears, but the hero mostly hands off. | Hero says Wine Bar + Provisions, shows proof, and keeps Reserve / Toast above the fold. |
| Toast and gift cards exist but compete with many Wix routes. | Toast order and gift cards are clear commercial actions. |
| OpenTable reputation exists off-site. | 4.7 OpenTable proof is surfaced as soft credibility, without overclaiming. |
| Wix footer / platform feel weakens premium confidence. | Bramble-style editorial warmth fits a wine-and-shared-plates room. |

## Demo Path

1. Show hero: wine-forward image cycle, Reserve, Toast order, OpenTable proof.
2. Open `/menu`: point out inline plates and wine specials plus preserved PDF fallbacks.
3. Show hours: bar/kitchen split is available before guests decide.
4. Show provider handoff: reservation opens existing `/res`; order opens live Toast.

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
| --- | --- |
| "We already use Toast." | "Perfect. I kept it. The site just gets more guests to Toast with less friction." |
| "Our menus change." | "The page preview can stay curated and seasonal; PDFs remain as the operational source until you want a CMS." |
| "We do not want to lose Wix forms." | "This v1 does not require replacing reservations, ordering, gift cards, or PDF operations." |
| "We are a wine bar, not a cocktail lounge." | "Exactly. This fork keeps Bramble's warmth but removes the cocktail-room posture." |
