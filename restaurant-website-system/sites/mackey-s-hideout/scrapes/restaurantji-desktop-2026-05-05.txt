About Mackey's Hideout

Mackey's Hideout is a classic dive bar with a great vibe, offering a quaint venue, friendly service, and a wide selection of drinks. Enjoy live music with excellent acoustics and lighting, and savor their frozen pizzas. This welcoming spot features slots, darts, and a stage for musicians, making it a top favorite among locals and visitors alike.
