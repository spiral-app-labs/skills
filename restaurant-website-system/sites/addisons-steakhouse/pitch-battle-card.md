# Addison's Steakhouse Battle Card

## Position

Addison's does not need a new personality. It needs the website to make the phone reservation, menu, from-scratch kitchen, and guest trust easier to believe in the first 30 seconds.

## One-Liner

"We kept Addison's phone-first reservation flow, then rebuilt the owned site around what guests already love: from-scratch steaks and seafood, regulars-room hospitality, and an easier mobile path to call."

## Show Order

1. **Before desktop:** `pitch-screenshots/before-desktop.png`  
   Point out `GReAT aMERICAN FOOD`, cluttered nav, and weak steakhouse signal.
2. **After desktop:** `pitch-screenshots/after-desktop.png`  
   Point out `From Scratch`, live open status, call CTA, and cleaner nav.
3. **After mobile:** `pitch-screenshots/after-mobile.png`  
   Point out tap-to-call above the fold and first-viewport clarity.
4. **Review proof:** `pitch-screenshots/review-desktop.png` and `review-mobile-cards.png`  
   Point out anonymous review proof, rating banner, and no reviewer names.
5. **Live demo:** `http://localhost:3020/menu` then `http://localhost:3020/contact`  
   Point out real menu categories, hours, map, phone, and visit details.

## Owner Objections

| Objection | Response |
| --- | --- |
| "Our phone reservations already work." | "Agreed. I did not replace them. The prototype makes the phone workflow easier on mobile with tap-to-call in the header, hero, sticky button, and contact page." |
| "We update Wix ourselves." | "That flexibility is useful. The issue is that the site now looks owner-managed: seasonal nav clutter, stale footer, and a generic hero. The new version keeps the content editable but gives the guest path a cleaner structure." |
| "We do not have pro photos." | "The prototype uses stock as a placeholder. One hero-quality ribeye shot, one warm room photo, and one chef/team photo would raise the whole site." |
| "We may be changing locations." | "Then the site should be built so address, map, hours, and location proof can change cleanly without rewriting the whole homepage." |
| "We don't want online booking." | "No problem. The prototype is phone-first. A booking widget can be added later, but it is not required for the current build." |

## Must-Ask Before Closing

- Confirm address and operating status.
- Confirm whether "Since 2017" is approved.
- Confirm whether Chef Michael can be named.
- Confirm whether owner wants employee names used or kept generalized.
- Ask for real photos before final delivery.

## Close

"The business already has the substance. The pitch is simply: let the website stop making guests work to see it."
