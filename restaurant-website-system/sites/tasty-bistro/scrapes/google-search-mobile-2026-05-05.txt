Tasty Bistro in Crystal Lake, IL, has closed its physical location and merged with its sister restaurant, Tasty Sushi in Cary, due to rising costs, with all staff and the menu moving to the Cary location. While the Crystal Lake restaurant is no longer operating, customers can still find the same food and staff at the Cary location, which is located at 630 Northwest Highway. The business is also considering public feedback on how to potentially reopen the Crystal Lake location in the future.
