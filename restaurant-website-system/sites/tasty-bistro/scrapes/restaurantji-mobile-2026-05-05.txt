About TASTY BISTRO sushi,ramen, thai,chinese .

TASTY BISTRO is a restaurant that offers a diverse menu, featuring sushi, ramen, Thai, and Chinese cuisine. The ramen, particularly the Tonkotsu Ramen, is praised for its fresh, creamy, and spicy flavors, making it a standout dish. The sushi is also highly regarded, with fresh fish, perfectly balanced rice, and an impressive selection of sake and liquor. The all-you-can-eat sushi for $28 provides good value and is considered one of the best in the Chicago area. Recent visitors enjoyed the welcoming atmosphere, describing it as a delightful and relaxed space, and appreciated the friendly staff, though some noted that service can be slow. The food generally receives positive comments, with large portions and great taste, highlighted by dishes like the Lucky Roll, teriyaki filet, gyoza, and crab Rangoon. The decor has a mix of styles, giving the place a slightly eclectic vibe, but the food remains top-notch. Overall, TASTY BISTRO offers a varied dining experience with some true highlights on the menu, making it a favorite destination for many.
