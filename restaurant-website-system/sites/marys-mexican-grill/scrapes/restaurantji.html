About Mary's Mexican Grill

Mary's Mexican Grill offers a clean and comfortable dining space, serving authentic Mexican cuisine with friendly and accommodating service at affordable prices. The menu features delicious options, including burritos suiza, chilaquiles verdes with chicken, and the highly praised Ceviche, made with extremely fresh ingredients and beautifully presented. Guests frequently enjoy the homemade guacamole, red and green sauces, and the street tacos, especially the pastor taco with cilantro, onion, and lime. The homemade tortillas are noted for their perfection and texture, enhancing the overall dining experience. With a lovely setting in the town square, it's a recommended spot to visit and support a local small business that deserves to be here for years to come. The restaurant has received praise for its excellent food and clean facilities, and many customers have expressed their satisfaction with the service, making it a popular choice for both locals and visitors.
