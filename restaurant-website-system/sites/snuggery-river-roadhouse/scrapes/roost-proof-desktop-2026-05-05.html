Snuggery River Roadhouse McHenry Menu – McHenry, IL

March 22, 2026

Type : Restaurant

Location : McHenry, IL

Open year round, this family owned waterfront restaurant, patio and private party house brings warm hospitality to McHenry locals and visitors.
Freshly made Snuggery favorites include fan fave broasted chicken, fish n chips, Prime NY strip steak, burgers, sandwiches, salads and Snug’s famous appetizers like Beer Nuggets, hand breaded Wisconsin Cheese Curds, our show stopping Nachos and Wings in 6 flavors. PLUS the addition of Moretti’s award winning Chicago style pizza, voted Best Pizza in Chicagoland’s largest diners poll, Daily Herald “Best of the Best” 2022.
Next door to the Snuggery is our Guest House a separate waterfront party house for private events.
Snuggery offers pick up orders, and delivery through major delivery apps.

Casual haunt featuring TVs tuned to sports, American grub & a big waterfront patio in relaxed digs. Based on 939 reviews, the restaurant has received a rating of 4.1 stars. Price $$

About us

Roost Café and Bistro offers a cozy atmosphere and eclectic regional cuisine, using local products whenever possible. Our affordable menu offers European-inspired plates, and guests can choose between a single entrée or multiple courses. In addition, our patio provides a delightful spot for alfresco dining during the warmer months. We are conveniently located between York and Ogunquit.
