Snuggery River Roadhouse

Year Round Food & Fun!

Welcome to the Snuggery River Roadhouse, where food, drinks, and fun meet on the scenic shores of the Fox River. Open year round, our family-owned roadhouse brings warm hospitality to both McHenry locals and visitors to the Chain O' Lakes.

Enjoy favorites like our famous Pressure Fried Crispy Chicken, hand-breaded Wisconsin Cheese Curds, sizzling wings, stacked burgers, and Moretti's award-winning Chicago-style pizza. With indoor and outdoor seating, a lively riverside patio, and plenty of flavor, every visit is a reason to relax and celebrate.
