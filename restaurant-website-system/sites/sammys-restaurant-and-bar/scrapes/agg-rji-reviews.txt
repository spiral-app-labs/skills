About Sammy's Restaurant & Bar

Sammy's Restaurant & Bar is a cozy, old-school diner that offers great food and service in a relaxed atmosphere. The menu features LARGE portions with great flavors, including a popular pretzel appetizer that's HUGE! It is a great local spot with delicious food, from excellent tater tots and perfectly cooked curly fries to a tasty pulled pork sandwich and wonderful vegetable soup. The friendly staff ensures that drinks are always full and smiles are plentiful. It is a very welcoming place with a terrific small town bar atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy live music, including local bands, and even dance the night away on the small dance floor. Parking is available nearby on Coral Street.
