Rocco Filippi 6 months ago on Google
So we take a motorcycle ride up to More Brewing in Huntley and run into this very nice barkeep. We told her where we were going to eat while in town. She suggested Sammy's. So we gave them a try.
What a delightful surprise. All 4 of us were happy with the quick service and the waitress was on it when we made changes to our order from what was on the menu. "No problem!" she told us every time we added or deleted something that came in our choices. Drinks were delivered asap. And when the food arrived I was thinking how I was going to try and eat all of it there was so much.
The whole time we were there she came over and asked us if we needed anything. My cup of coffee never got empty. And her personality was right up there with the old "down home small town" theme. We had a wonderful conversation with her. She introduced us to not only the other waitress as we went along but with all the talk of how we really enjoyed the food and service she had the owner/cook come out and we forwarded our opinion to him and expressed how we felt. I then told them that I HAD to leave a review with context to the entire experience.
I would have to say as many greasy spoons as I have been to this one is at the top of my list. And the people with me would agree to that I am sure. If your in the mood for some great breakfast with good people and are really hungry this is the place for you. Oh, and did I say the prices were actually lower for once than what we received in return?!
