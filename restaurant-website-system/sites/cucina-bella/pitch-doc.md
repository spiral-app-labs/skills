# Cucina Bella Pitch Doc

## 60-Second Read

Cucina Bella already has what most Italian restaurants try to manufacture: a real Sannicandro family story, generous red-sauce cooking, warm service, Toast reservations, catering, sister venues, and strong public proof.

The leak is that the site hides the proof and practical answers. Guests do not see the Tripadvisor authority, hours, address, phone, owner story, or an indexable menu in the order they need them.

The prototype keeps the family-casual Italian register and makes the secret sauce visible: bold garlic, big portions, family warmth, brick-room comfort, dessert/bar energy, and a table that keeps growing.

## Call Opener

"Your site has the reservation path, but it hides the proof and the family story that earned it; I mocked the version where Sannicandro, big portions, reviews, menu, hours, and Toast all work together."

## Where The Current Site Leaks Revenue

| Leak | Why It Matters | Standard Practice | Prototype Fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Public proof is hidden. | The audit found Tripadvisor authority that can reduce first-visit hesitation. | Put credible proof near the first decision point. | Hero and proof strip surface the rating/ranking as owner-confirm/re-verify content. |
| Menu depends on client-side loading. | Search, AI, and no-JS crawlers may not see item names, descriptions, or prices. | Serve menu text directly on the site. | Added a crawlable menu structure ready for owner-verified items. |
| Sannicandro story is buried. | The family origin is the most ownable brand asset. | Put the story right after the first promise. | Homepage now anchors the second section around the Sannicandro family line. |
| Hours/address/phone are too hard to find. | Guests deciding tonight need logistics before they reserve. | Show visit basics in hero, visit area, footer, and mobile actions. | Added open-status, visit section, click-to-call, and Toast handoff. |
| Review themes are not shaping the pitch. | Guests love value, portions, garlic, staff, events, and desserts. | Echo what guests actually remember. | Review cards and copy now emphasize garlic, portions, service, catering, and carrot cake/bar cues. |

## What We Fixed

- **Secret sauce:** The site now sounds like Cucina Bella: Sannicandro roots, red sauce, bold garlic, generous portions, warm staff, and family meals at fair value.
- **Proof:** Public reputation appears near the top instead of being left to third-party platforms.
- **Menu/search:** The menu path becomes readable and crawlable, not just a hydrated shell.
- **Preserve-stack:** Toast reservations and sister-venue links stay in place.

## Demo Path

1. Show the hero: family-run Italian, Sannicandro line, proof, Toast reserve, and visit basics.
2. Scroll to story, proof, reviews, and menu to show why the restaurant feels known.
3. Open the menu/visit route and point out Toast preservation plus owner-confirm menu/hours placeholders.

## Do Not Overclaim

- Re-verify Tripadvisor numbers before outreach.
- Do not claim a founding year or named award until the owner confirms.
- Do not present prototype hours, menu details, or prices as final.
- Treat Google review excerpts as direction unless owner approves testimonial use.

## Close

"This is not making Cucina Bella into something fancier; it is making the website show the family meal people already come back for."
