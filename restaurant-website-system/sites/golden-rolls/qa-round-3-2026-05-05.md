## What is world-class already

- **Identity:** The site feels specific to Golden Rolls: Woodstock, Eastwood Drive, hidden-gem interior, fresh generous sushi, creative rolls, hot Japanese entrées, and attentive service all show up quickly.
- **Conversion:** The owner-demo paths are obvious: call, menu, directions, address, official site, and conservative current-availability handoffs. The site does not invent online reservations or provider-specific delivery links.
- **Accuracy posture:** High-risk facts are either sourced from captured audit/review/menu evidence or explicitly routed to phone/current verification. The menu page now reminds guests to call for current prices, specials, hours, and delivery options.
- **Design quality:** The dark green / cream / amber palette, serif display type, rounded cards, and sushi imagery feel polished enough to sell while staying casual-local instead of fake luxury omakase.
- **Mobile polish:** CDP evidence confirms no horizontal overflow across home, menu, about, and contact at 320px, 390px, and 1440px. Mobile nav is tight but usable; CTAs remain clear.
