## Final verdict

Pass after fixes. Golden Rolls is sellable for the next QA gate, with no visible horizontal overflow at 320px, 390px, or desktop widths and no hard factual/sellability blocker found in this round.

## What is world-class already

- **Identity:** The site quickly feels like Golden Rolls specifically: Woodstock/Eastwood Drive, hidden-gem sushi room, fresh generous rolls, attentive service, and practical dine-in/takeout/delivery context.
- **Conversion:** Call, menu, directions, address, phone, and official-site paths are visible and repeated across high-intent pages.
- **Accuracy posture:** The site avoids fake reservation paths, fake chef/omakase claims, fake provider promises, and allergy certainty. Time-sensitive questions route to phone.
- **Design quality:** Dark green, cream, gold, serif display type, sushi imagery, and rounded card rhythm create a polished bistro feel without drifting into formal omakase.
- **Mobile polish:** CDP evidence confirms `scrollWidth == clientWidth` at 320px, 390px, and 1440px for home, menu, about, and contact.

## What blocked sellability during QA round 1

Initial QA capture surfaced a narrow-mobile risk on the contact page: long contact-card text and the official URL could wrap/crop awkwardly at 320px. This mattered because phone, address, directions, and official site are core conversion content.

## Critical fixes completed before Ethan sees it

1. Added an explicit viewport export in `app/layout.tsx` so mobile rendering is locked to device width.
2. Added global horizontal overflow guards in `app/globals.css`.
3. Tightened contact-page cards for narrow mobile:
   - added `min-w-0`
   - reduced mobile card padding
   - reduced contact-card display text from `text-3xl` to responsive `text-2xl sm:text-3xl`
   - added `break-words` to phone/address/official-site and quick-detail rows
4. Replaced the slightly over-fancy phrase “white-tablecloth energy” with the more accurate “a surprisingly polished dining room.”
5. Re-ran production `typecheck` and `next build` after fixes.
6. Captured CDP screenshots and DOM snapshots for home, menu, about, and contact at 320px, 390px, and desktop.
