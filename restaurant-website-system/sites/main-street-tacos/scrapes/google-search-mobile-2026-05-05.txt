Main Street Tacos is a casual Mexican restaurant in Wauconda, Illinois, known for its authentic, made-to-order tacos, burritos, and other comfort food, with popular items including fish, steak, and chorizo tacos, and guacamole. It offers counter service with food delivered to your table, and while it's not a fancy sit-down place, many find the food tasty and reasonably priced, though some reviews mention inconsistent service and a lack of free chips and salsa.
