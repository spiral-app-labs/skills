About

Main Street Tacos

Welcome to Main Street Tacos, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Wauconda, Illinois. Located at 327 N Main St, this eatery embodies the spirit of authentic Mexican cuisine with a unique twist. Here, you can indulge in vibrant flavors served in an inviting, laid-back atmosphere. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, this taco haven promises a culinary experience that you won't soon forget.

The concept at Main Street Tacos is refreshingly straightforward. You step up to the counter, order your food, and then take a seat while your meal is freshly prepared. It’s a no-frills setup that focuses on what truly matters: the food. As one patron sums it up perfectly, “it's a true hole-in-the-wall joint.” For those who enjoy the charm of local businesses, this spot checks all the boxes—fantastic food at reasonable prices, a welcoming environment, and the thrill of discovering what a family-owned eatery has to offer.

Tacos that Impress: Diners rave about the crispy, hard-shell tacos that are filled with generous portions of meticulously prepared meats. From the deliciously marinated chicken to the adventurous beef tongue, there’s something for every palate.
Affordability: With tacos priced at around $2.50 each and soft drinks starting at just $1.25, indulging in an array of dishes without breaking the bank is delightfully easy.
Flavor and Quality: Reviewers consistently highlight the small, diced meat cubes that take taco enjoyment to the next level. This attention to preparation ensures each bite bursts with flavor.
Real Mexican Flavors: From fish to chicken tacos and generous burritos, Main Street Tacos guarantees authenticity. Customers appreciate the option to customize their orders, making this spot a favorite for both traditionalists and those wishing to explore more unique offerings.

When you step into Main Street Tacos, you're not just enjoying a meal; you're participating in a local experience steeped in community. Don’t forget to check out their colorful handwritten specials, particularly the enticing tamales when they’re available. Mark Beemsterboer, a satisfied customer, expresses it best: "Please support independent! Please support local! Please support family businesses! Please support Main Street Tacos!"

With a commitment to quality, a captivating culinary journey awaits at Main Street Tacos. Whether you're a taco aficionado or a newcomer to the world of authentic Mexican food, prepare to leave with a happy heart and a full stomach.

So, come on down, grab a seat, and treat yourself to what many already consider the best Mexican cuisine in Wauconda—because at Main Street Tacos, every meal tells a delicious story.

“ Fantastic. Solid. REAL. If any of that means something to you, visit Main Street Tacos. Here is what you do. You order at the counter, right when you walk in. (You will stop at the counter to pay, when you leave.) Then you find a table. Occupy it. Someone will bring your beverages and meal to you. No one will bring you your bill; it will be waiting for you at the counter when you leave, stuffed to the eyeballs with tacos. It's easy. It's a true hole-in-the-wall joint. A gem. The food is fantastic, very reasonably priced. The dining room is a step above dining in a room full of picnic tables, as there are actual chairs. This might sound critical, but I hope it never changes. It's a weirdo set up, they use a weirdo order-taking system, but it works for them. And it works for the good people of Wauconda. Price changes happen via stickers placed over older pricing, and specials are advertised on colorful hand-written signs, mostly in Spanish. "Hay tamales" means "there are tamales," which is a signal that you might want to order some. Do that. The menu is mostly a la carte, though there are some dinners that come with rice and beans. I seriously cannot wait to go back. Please support independent! Please support local! Please support family businesses! Please support Main Street Tacos! ”

- Mark Beemsterboer

“ Went here for the first time expecting some incredible tacos, and I was not disappointed. I got the chicken, marinated steak, and tried the beef tongue, all hard shell and authentic style. The chicken blew me away, it was amazing. The marinated steak was okay, they definitely need to add some flavor in, like Tabasco sauce. The beef tongue had little flavor, but it was not bad (I would not get it again, personally). The shells are crispy and tasty with great crunch, like On The Border's chips. The best part is how they process the meat. It comes as tiny diced cubes, as does the onion, which puts it all above a five stars for me. Tiny, smaller-than-bite-sized cubes of meat makes the entire experience of eating a taco next level, and will be coming back to try some more of their menu options and tacos! I guess the even better part for some is the incredibly low prices they have for their fresh food. A bottle water is only 1.25, and each taco is around 2.50 each. They also sell Monster energy drinks of all things, which I think would go really well with the meaty tacos. ”

- Ryan M.
