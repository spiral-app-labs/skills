## Why I thought this was worth building

- V's House already has a stronger story than most restaurants: Vu family heritage, public press, strong review volume, and a room guests repeatedly call beautiful.
- Your menu has range — pho, sushi, banh khot, shaken beef, boba, cocktails — but the current site makes guests work too hard to scan it.
- The preview keeps the operational paths you already use: Toast reservations, Toast ordering, phone, directions, gift cards, Instagram/Facebook.

## What I changed in the preview

- Led with the family story instead of a generic welcome line.
- Added proof above the fold: Google/OpenTable ratings, press, and named dish/service signals.
- Rebuilt the menu as structured website content instead of image-only menu browsing.
- Kept the tone polished but casual — not fine dining, not a fake brand, just V's House presented with the same care as the room.

## A few things to confirm before anything goes live

- Are the menu prices currently shown from your official menu image set still current?
- Any dishes you would rather feature first: Beef Deluxe Pho, Banh Khot, Shaken Beef, toasted crab bread, cocktails, boba, or something else?

No pressure and no deck — I just wanted to show what the site could look like if the story guests already love was front and center.
