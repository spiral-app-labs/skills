## Final verdict

Pass for QA Round 1. The preview is not yet delivered, but Round 1 blockers were found, fixed, rebuilt, and re-evidenced. The site is now strong enough to advance into QA Round 2.

## What is working

- Identity: still reads as a warm East Dundee neighborhood Mexican restaurant, not a fake upscale concept.
- Conversion: Call, Menu, Order, and Map are visible on mobile and the contact page preserves phone, address, hours, menu, directions, and order handoffs.
- Accuracy: phone `(847) 426-0506`, address `411 E Main St, East Dundee, IL 60118`, hours, menu link, directions link, and order link are present in the checked DOM/API surfaces.
- Mobile polish: 390px checks found no horizontal overflow/offenders across home, menu, contact, and concierge-open states.
- Concierge: deterministic API responses covered menu recommendations, hours, pickup, allergy handoff, and location; allergy/ingredient-sensitive questions route to phone.

## Round 1 blockers found and fixed

1. Decorative SVG cards were cropped on mobile and felt too proof/internal for guests.
   - Fixed by hiding menu/blog decorative image cards on mobile and switching decorative image rendering to `object-contain` where visible.
   - Replaced visible internal labels such as “Taco Proof,” “Menu Range,” “Current path,” and “Source-backed.”

2. Several visitor-facing labels still sounded internal/template-like.
   - Rewrote “Google Review Packet” to “Local Guest Notes.”
   - Rewrote “Use the links guests actually need” to “Call, map, menu, or order in one tap.”
   - Rewrote contact/footer labels such as “Full menu link,” “Pickup order link,” and “Built for quick calls...” into guest-facing copy.

3. Initial QA image detection showed broken SVG images caused by unescaped ampersands in SVG text.
   - Escaped SVG ampersands and reran DOM/image checks.
   - Final check reports 0 visible broken images across tested desktop/mobile states.

4. Concierge/mobile floating CTA could feel more prominent than menu/order actions.
   - Reduced mobile concierge trigger size and moved it above the bottom action strip.
   - Open chat modal overlap is expected only after the guest opens the concierge.
