About La Hacienda Mexican Restaurant

La Hacienda Mexican Restaurant offers authentic Mexican cuisine, featuring tasty burritos, flavorful tacos, and delicious tortas. The presentation of the food is impressive, and the salsa and guacamole are highly praised. Although parking can be a bit challenging, the restaurant has handicap spaces in front and ample seating inside. The torta with grilled chicken and red sauce is a must-try. The owners are described as sweet and nice, and the service is praised as attentive, knowledgeable, and polite. Customers highly recommend the restaurant, especially the chicken burritos and the chicken nuggets, which are considered the most delicious in the world.
