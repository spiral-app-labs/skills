    Ultimate AYCE Experience! Grill, Dip & Sushi Feast!

    MAAX Asian BBQ & Hot Pots is a modern Asian Hot Pot and BBQ restaurant that brings the rich, interactive dining traditions of East Asia to the heart of Arlington Heights. We offer a vibrant, immersive culinary experience where guests cook their own meats, vegetables, and noodles in simmering broths or over smokeless grills built into each table.


    Our menu features a curated blend of traditional and modern flavors from Chinese, Japanese, Korean and Thai cuisines, including premium meats, fresh seafood, house-made dipping sauces, and vegetarian-friendly options. Whether you're craving a comforting hot pot in the winter or sizzling BBQ any time of year, MAAX is the perfect destination for groups, families, and food enthusiasts.
