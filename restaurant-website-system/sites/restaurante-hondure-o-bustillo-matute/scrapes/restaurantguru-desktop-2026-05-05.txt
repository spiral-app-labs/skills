Recently tried Restaurante Hondureño Bustillo Matute in McHenry and it was AMAZING! My friend and I decided to give this new spot a try for dinner, and we were pleasantly surprised. We started with glass bottle coke (the best coke) and they even brought out complimentary fried plantains with sweet cream & sugar (check out the 2nd picture). Let me tell you, those plantains took me to another planet with just one bite! For our main course, we ordered the carne asada (1st picture) and Tacos Dorados De Pollo (crispy tacos with chicken). The carne asada was delicious, with fresh veggies, amazing corn tortillas (made in-house), and beans that I couldn't stop raving about. Trust me, you'll want to try them! Pro tip: ask for the verde sauce, it's spicy and delicious. As for the Tacos Dorados, they were like flautas and were excellent. The crispy tortilla with tender chicken and the red sauce was a flavor explosion! My only regrets were not coming here sooner and not trying the papusas. Overall, a solid 10 out of 10! If you're in McHenry, you've got to check this place out!
