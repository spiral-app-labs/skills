  • (November 25, 2023, 6:01 pm)

    I want to preface that I am Honduran and of course, I know a taste from "home". If you're reading this review, expect authentic flavor but also, unique to their style. Fresh flour tortillas made by hand, for the baleadas. I also tried the pollo con tajadas, it is the traditional taste from this street style popular dish. I bought the mantequilla (sour cream) and queso (cheese) by the pound, it is delicious. Prices are fair, considering the size of their portions. Also, considering in 1 baleada you can be eating a full meal. My best wishes for this family on entrepreneurs in the McHenry area.

    P.S: Upgrades are coming soon, per the owners. They are still working out some details. This is just from my interaction with them.
