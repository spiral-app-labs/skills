## Secret Sauce

- Owner/site claim: Cucina Bella is a family-run Italian-American restaurant rooted in Tony's Sannicandro, Italy family story, with Toast reservations, sister venues, catering, and a warm Main Street Algonquin room.
- Review-confirmed strengths: bold garlic/red-sauce cooking, generous portions, warm staff and owner presence, fair value for the amount of food, brick-room atmosphere, dessert martinis/bar energy, and event/catering coordination.
- Named items or experiences: Stuffed Mushrooms, bruschetta, Nonna's Meat Lasagna, Chicken Saltimbocca, Chicken Parm, pork, salmon, carrot cake, dessert martinis, baby shower planning with Erin, and family/sister-venue cross-links.
- Guest language worth echoing: "bold garlic," "family meal," "huge portions," "local spot," "warm and attentive," "reasonable for the quality," and "table that keeps growing."
- Risks / do-not-overclaim: Tripadvisor proof from the 2026-04-27 audit should be re-verified before outreach. Do not claim a founding year or named award until the owner confirms. The prototype includes representative menu and hour assumptions that need owner approval.
- Website/pitch implications: The pitch should make the owner feel seen for the family story and guest generosity, not just criticized for hidden proof. The new site should surface Sannicandro, portions/value, red-sauce comfort, reviews, hours, and Toast in the first decision path.
