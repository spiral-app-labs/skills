I decided to try a New Mexican Restaurant today and the previous reviews made this an easy decision. The outside is the restaurant is clean and has a sign that easily identifies the restaurant.

The inside of the restaurant was clean. I only wished they had some tables to enjoy my food inside instead of in my car.
