## What regulars love

1. **Authentic Veracruz / regional Mexican identity.** Reviewers explicitly call out “authentic comida Veracruzana,” Vera Cruz style Mexican food, sope Veracruzano, garnachas, flautas, empanadas, mole, and huachinango a la veracruzana.
2. **Tacos + burritos as repeat-visit anchors.** Tacos are repeatedly called the best in the area / Crystal Lake; steak tacos, pork tacos with creamy green sauce, shrimp tacos, carne asada tacos, lengua tacos, steak burrito dinner, and super burrito all surface.
3. **Takeout done carefully.** Multiple reviews praise speed, accurate orders, careful packaging, included napkins/hot sauce/silverware, and carryout value.
4. **Friendly, family-feeling service.** Reviewers repeatedly mention friendly/super friendly/nice staff, being greeted quickly, and being treated warmly even near closing.
5. **Clean, modest hidden-gem storefront.** The space is described as clean, well maintained, modest, and a hidden gem; the main drawback is limited/no sit-down seating.
6. **Fair prices and fresh ingredients.** Guests cite value (`3 tacos for $6`, burrito dinner around $11, good price-to-quality) and say the food tastes fresh, scratch-made, and not cheaped-out.

- Homepage menu highlights should feature tacos, empanadas, sope Veracruzano, garnachas, huarache de asado, mole/atole specials, burritos, and churros.
- Conversion should be phone/maps/takeout-first, with honest note that dine-in/seating is limited or evolving unless verified otherwise.
