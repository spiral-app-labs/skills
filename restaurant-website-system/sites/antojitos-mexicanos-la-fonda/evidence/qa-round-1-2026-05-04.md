# Antojitos Mexicanos La Fonda — QA Round 1 (2026-05-04)

## Final verdict

Pass after fixes. QA Round 1 found sellability risks around source/date clarity, truth-safe wording, mobile footer heaviness, and post-footer spacing. Those were corrected in this round; no remaining hard blocker was found in the final mobile check.

## What is working well

- **Identity:** The site feels specific to La Fonda: Veracruz-style antojitos, tacos, sope Veracruzano, empanadas, huaraches, mole/atole, clean carryout, friendly service, and Crystal Lake proof all show up in the page.
- **Conversion:** Primary paths are obvious and accurate: call, directions, Google profile, and truth-safe concierge. Mobile sticky CTAs keep call/directions within one tap.
- **Accuracy:** Phone, address, publicly listed hours, 4.5/148 Google proof, and 30-review packet language are tied to captured/public-source evidence. No fake online ordering or full menu/pricing was added.
- **Design quality:** The bright `pepper-01` structure remains lively and takeout-forward; proof strip + menu cards + concierge give the page enough density to feel sellable.
- **Mobile polish:** Final mobile pass has `scrollWidth=390`, no horizontal offenders, footer reaches the bottom cleanly, and sticky CTA does not create a white post-footer gap.

## QA findings fixed in this round

1. **Source/date clarity for no-site and stats claims**
   - Changed broad `No owned site` wording to safer `No official site found` / `2026-05-04 Google Maps capture showed Add website` wording.
   - Added source/date labels around Google rating, review count, and Add website proof.

2. **Truth-safe menu/order wording**
   - Changed internal/overstrong language from `fake` claims to `unverified` provider/pricing language.
   - Renamed `Tacos People Reorder` to `Tacos Regulars Mention` to avoid implying transaction data.
   - Clarified that menu highlights come from public review/listing evidence and that guests should call to verify today’s menu, prices, and specials.

3. **Mobile footer and post-footer spacing**
   - Compressed the mobile footer hours into one public-source line.
   - Moved sticky-CTA spacing into the red footer padding instead of adding white body padding.
   - Final visual check confirmed the blank post-footer band is gone.

4. **Contact wording**
   - Changed `Current web gap` to `Current website gap` and kept hours framed as publicly listed plus call-to-confirm for holiday changes.
