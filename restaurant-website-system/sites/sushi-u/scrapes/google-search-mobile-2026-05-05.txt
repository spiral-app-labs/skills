Sushi U is a Japanese restaurant in Crystal Lake, IL, known for its fresh ingredients, diverse menu, and options for dine-in, takeout, and delivery, including sushi, ramen, hibachi, and poke bowls. They offer lunch specials, an "all you can eat" option, and a variety of rolls, with a focus on healthier preparation methods. Popular items include the Spicy Tuna Roll, Shrimp Tempura Roll, and various poke bowls.
