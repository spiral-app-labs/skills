# Routing Template Decision — 2026-05-06

- archetype: `Cuisine`
- template_slug: `plate-01`

## Rationale

`Maxfield's Pancake House` is a warm, casual, neighborhood breakfast restaurant where the primary conversion path is practical: call, directions, and menu confidence. The `Cuisine` archetype fits because it prioritizes approachable structure, readable menu density, and clear visit information over fine-dining theater.

`plate-01` stays appropriate as the structural base because its hero-to-inline-menu rhythm works well for a breakfast house with recognizable public-source dish signals. The build keeps that structure but rewrites the voice, CTAs, hours handling, proof sections, and color register to feel like a Schaumburg pancake house instead of a generic urban bistro.
