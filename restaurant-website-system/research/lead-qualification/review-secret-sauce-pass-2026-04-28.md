## Sales Pattern

The strongest version of this process has two beats:

1. Diagnose the site leak with specificity: PDF menu, dead menu path, hidden proof, weak reservation/order path, split domains, directory-like experience, or poor mobile decision flow.
2. Immediately name what the restaurant already does well: staff warmth, signature dishes, room vibe, value, events, wine program, owner story, or special service moments.

That second beat is what makes the owner feel seen. It also gives the prototype better copy: we are not just fixing navigation; we are making the site express what guests already remember.
