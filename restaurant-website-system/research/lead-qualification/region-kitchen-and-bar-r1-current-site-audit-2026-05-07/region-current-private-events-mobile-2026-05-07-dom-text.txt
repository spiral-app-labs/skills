Private Events

Region is the premier celebration destination for the Northwest Suburbs.

Swanky and chic, this locale is a stunner but the exemplary cuisine and attentive but refined service are sure to surpass your expectations each and every visit.

We can accommodate private dining events for groups of 15 to 170 guests, making us the perfect location for business dinners, social gatherings, rehearsal dinners and any and all other events. Our private dining room can accommodate up to 40 guests for a seated dinner and the outside patio can hold up to 30 guests for cocktails and dinner. The entire restaurant is also available for full buy-outs and can accommodate up to 170 guests.
