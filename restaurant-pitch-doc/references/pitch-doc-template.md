# Restaurant Pitch Doc Template

Use this shape unless the user asks for a different one.

```markdown
# {Restaurant} Pitch Doc

## 60-Second Read
{2-4 sentences. Say what already works, where revenue/pre-dining intent leaks, and what the redesign fixed.}

## Call Opener
"{one sentence the seller can say out loud}"

## Where The Current Site Leaks Revenue
| Leak | Why It Matters | Standard Practice | Prototype Fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| {specific leak} | {guest/business consequence} | {what a good restaurant site should do} | {what we built} |

## What We Fixed
- **{Fix area}:** {specific change and business effect}
- **{Fix area}:** {specific change and business effect}
- **{Fix area}:** {specific change and business effect}

## Demo Path
1. {First screen to show and why}
2. {Second scroll/page and why}
3. {Provider handoff or conversion action to prove preserve-stack}

## Do Not Overclaim
- {Unverified credential, uncertain metric, provider replacement risk, or current-site strength to respect}

## Close
"{one sentence that frames next step or asks for reaction}"
```

Rules:

- Keep the doc under roughly 900 words.
- Use tables for leak/fix mapping; use bullets for call notes.
- If evidence came from an audit, use confident language.
- If evidence is inferred from prototype code or general best practice, say "likely" or "the safer pitch is."
- Never pitch replacing working restaurant commerce providers unless the build actually does that and the user asked for it.
